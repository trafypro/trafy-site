Tech companies are setting incredible new standards for their talent acquisition process. This might speed up our human evolution process but will also put the survival of people who put minimum effort in upskilling at stake.

Here are a few things you could do that will keep you relevant in this evolving market.

1. Learn the Working Principles of Artificial Intelligence

There are a lot of YouTube channels and podcasts, that discuss the history and possible predictions in AI market. This will help you understand the game.

Suggestion:

https://www.youtube.com/@IBMTechnology

2. Keep track of new AI tools

Every day there is a new company emerges in the AI market. Most of these companies offer a free solution with limited features. But that’s more than enough to understand what they try to do and how they position themselves.

Suggestion:

https://www.producthunt.com/

3. Network with Independent Contributors

Independent contributors are the most essential people who help any technology to evolve. Almost all tech companies keep their certain projects open-source to gain inputs from Independent Contributors.

Suggestion:

Linkedin, X

4. Drop the conventional mindset

As we age at some point we conclude that enough is enough. And this is the reason most people like to get stagnant at some point in their life. Be open and optimistic about the future. More than ever it look bright.

Suggestion:

Read tech news this will help you to achieve the paradigm shift.

5. Learn to code simple data models

This may sound pretty hard, but everything has a beginning. Data science has now become a cross-domain skillset as it has become inevitable to analyse and predict possible outcomes in every industry.

Suggestion:

https://www.python.org/
